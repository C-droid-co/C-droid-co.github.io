Glossary & Trader Slang

CALL → You want stock to go UP.
PUT → You want stock to go DOWN.
STRIKE → The price your option controls.
PREMIUM → Price you pay for the option.
EXPIRATION → Option's deadline.
ITM / OTM / ATM → In / Out / At the money.
GREEKS → Delta, Theta, Gamma, Vega.
OPEN INTEREST → Contracts open (liquidity).
SWEEP → Big aggressive buy (usually institutions).
FOMO → Fear of missing out.
BAGHOLDER → Stuck in losing trade.
WICKED OUT → Stopped out by wick spike.
ROLLING → Move position to later expiry.
PUMP / RUG → Hype moves / fake breakouts.
