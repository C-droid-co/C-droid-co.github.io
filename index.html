Options Trading 101

Lesson 1: What Is An Option? (Calls & Puts)

  • Options give right, not obligation to buy/sell stock.
  • Call = bullish, Put = bearish.
  • Small moves in stock → BIG moves in options.

Lesson 2: Option Contract Anatomy

  • Strike Price → The price you control
  • Expiration Date → Contract deadline
  • Premium → Price to enter
  • Greeks → Advanced (covered later)
